# Business Questions Catalog

## Sales

- What are total sales?
- What were sales last month?
- What is the average order value?
- Which month generated the most revenue?
- How many orders are pending?

## Customers

- How many customers do we have?
- Who are our top customers?
- Which country has the most customers?
- Which industry generates the most revenue?

## Products

- What is the top-selling product?
- Which product generated the most revenue?
- Which category performs best?

## Finance

- What is the value of unpaid invoices?
- How many overdue invoices exist?
- What percentage of invoices are paid?
- How much revenue has been collected?

## Employees

- How many employees do we have?
- Which department has the most employees?
- Who joined this year?

## Support

- How many tickets are open?
- How many tickets are in progress?
- Which customers have the most tickets?
